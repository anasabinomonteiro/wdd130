Forum & Community Profiles

On this page, you will have access to a vibrant and collaborative community. Our forum is a space where adults, especially women, who are considering transitioning into the Information Technology (IT) field after age 30, can connect, share experiences and exchange valuable advice. Here, you will find in-depth profiles of the women who generously contribute to enriching our community. They bring a wealth of experience and knowledge, offering a unique insight into the challenges and triumphs of changing careers into IT.

By exploring these profiles, you'll have the opportunity to connect with mentors and peers who have already walked this path, finding inspiration and guidance for your own journey. We believe that the power of community is fundamental to success, which is why we are committed to providing a welcoming and collaborative environment for everyone looking to transform their IT careers.
